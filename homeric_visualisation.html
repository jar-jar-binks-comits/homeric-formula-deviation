Homeric Formula Deviation Analysis

Computational Detection of Pattern-Breaking at Narrative Climaxes

Hypothesis: Homer exploits predictive processing by breaking formulaic patterns at emotionally significant moments. When expected formula X doesn't appear, the prediction error marks narrative importance—analogous to Dante's deployment of Beatrice's rhyme in Inferno V.

Method: Information-theoretic analysis. Surprisal = -log₂(P(formula|context)). High surprisal = deviation from expected patterns.
